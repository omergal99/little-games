🌎 Interests 🌎

The world divides into three for me

🎨 Creativity

Creativity is a huge field, and it is undoubtedly an important component of the development of the world. Creativity is created from different situations which we wish to solve, change, improve, and thus think of a way to do so. Creativity is measured by how many ways to do it, and by comparing them. Some guidelines are branching thinking, thinking out of the box, multi-dimensional thinking and multi-directional, and no fear of failure - failure is also necessary 🎨

🚪 Openness

Openness is one of the most powerful tools a person can achieve in life. Openness at a high level means a basic understanding of the world foundations , including a world-view and a system-realistic vision. One of them is: Not to think primarily about yourself, along with reducing the ego, by looking at our environment and taking an interest in it. This can still be talked for hours, and a small hint for your continuation, is the understanding that every person is a very minor part of our universe 🚪

🔆 Positivity

Positive posits an idea in which reality always occurs for the better. We create our reality, which usually comes from our thoughts, and positive thoughts come from loving yourself as you are, as well as accepting the environment as it is. And thus the essence of our world, which is positive, growing and prosperity, enriching, and enjoyable. This is one of the ways to 'touch happiness' which is finally our choice 🔆
